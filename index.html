About Me

Hi, I'm Urvish 👋

I'm an Indian student living in Germany with a deep passion for mathematics, physics, and computer science. I love building things that sit at the intersection of rigorous science and clean code.

Whether it's simulating orbital mechanics, preparing for math olympiads, or training machine-learning models, I approach every problem with curiosity and a desire to understand the why behind every result.

When I'm not coding I'm studying competition mathematics, reading about astrophysics, or working on the next science project for Jugend forscht.

Long-term goal: combine physics, math, and AI to tackle real scientific challenges — and (someday) universities like MIT. 🚀
